关于我们

TREAL GROUP 总部位于加拿大蒙特利尔，是一家专注于供应链管理和销售的行业领导者。自成立以来，公司始终致力于与北美高端品牌的深度合作，凭借多年的行业经验和强大的资源整合能力，在市场中建立了稳固的地位。

作为Natural Factors（NF）在中国的独家总代理，TREAL GROUP 致力于将北美最优质的保健品带给中国消费者。Natural Factors 以其严格的有机生产标准和可持续发展的理念享誉全球。通过这项深度合作，我们满足了中国消费者对有机、健康、可持续生活方式的追求，带来全新的健康体验。

TREAL GROUP 凭借精准的市场定位、卓越的运营策略和专业团队的协作，不断在供应链管理领域创新，确保高效、稳定的产品供应，并提供卓越的客户服务体验。

未来，TREAL GROUP 将继续秉持“有机健康，惠及未来”的核心理念，携手Natural Factors 将高品质、可持续发展的产品引入中国市场。我们以专业、创新、卓越的精神，为消费者提供值得信赖的产品和卓越的购物体验，助力健康生活方式的推广。

About Us

TREAL GROUP IS A SUPPLY CHAIN MANAGEMENT AND SALES COMPANY FROM MONTREAL, CANADA.

Headquartered in Montreal, Canada, TREAL GROUP is an industry leader specializing in supply chain management and sales. Since its inception, the company has committed to deep collaborations with top North American brands, leveraging extensive industry experience and robust resource integration to establish a solid position in the market.

As the exclusive distributor of Natural Factors (NF) in China, TREAL GROUP is dedicated to bringing the highest quality North American health supplements to Chinese consumers. Renowned for its rigorous organic production standards and commitment to sustainable development, Natural Factors embodies excellence in health and wellness. Through this strategic partnership, TREAL GROUP meets the growing demand for organic, healthy, and sustainable lifestyles, providing an unparalleled health experience to consumers.

TREAL GROUP’s precise market positioning, exceptional operational strategies, and collaborative professional team have enabled continuous innovation in supply chain management. This ensures efficient and reliable product supply and an unmatched customer service experience.

Looking ahead, TREAL GROUP will continue to uphold its core philosophy, “Organic Health, Benefiting the Future.” In collaboration with Natural Factors, we strive to introduce premium, sustainable products to the Chinese market. With a commitment to professionalism, innovation, and excellence, we aim to provide trustworthy products and an outstanding shopping experience, fostering the adoption of healthy lifestyles.
